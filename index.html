About Me

Hello! My name is Denart Ifurung. My background is in biology, organic chemistry, and medicine. Since I was young, I have always gravitated towards areas of study that involve the diagnosis of a problem and finding its resolution. That is very likely why I find myself currently delving into the world of computer programming and web development. It is the combination of technical analysis and problem solving, combined with the opportunity to create something that uniquely your own that fascinates me. While this may be a new direction for me, with its own set of distinctive challenges, I look forward to taking this journey, wherever it leads.

Interests

Though I am interested in programming and web development as a whole, both my academic and personal health experiences have led to a particular focus in healthcare. In medicine, communication is of paramount importance. Whether it is between the patients, doctors, their staff, or even between medical devices and diagnostic tools, there needs to be a robust, efficient line of communication in order to ensure the patient's best interests. The internet and web communication are quickly becoming cornerstones for this. The challenges we faced the past few years only exacerbated this need and revealed the importance remote communication can have in healthcare. As such, I would love to work within the healthcare industry developing solutions that streamline these lines of communications. I believe that technology not only has the potential to make things more convenient for medical professionals, beyond that I am truly convinced that it can have a positive impact on patient morbidity and mortality, and improve clinical outcomes in general.
