About Me

Hello! My name is Denart Ifurung. I have a background in biology, organic chemistry, and medicine. Through the years I have always gravitated towards subjects that involve the diagnosis of a problem and finding its resolution. That is very likely why I find myself currently delving into the world of computer programming and web development. It is the combination of technical analysis and problem solving, combined with the opportunity to create something that uniquely your own that fascinates me. While this may be a new direction for me, with its own set of distinctive challenges, I look forward to taking this journey, wherever it takes me.

Interests

Though I am interested in programming and web development as a whole, both my personal and academic experiences have led to a particular interest in healthcare. In medicine, communication is of paramount importance. Whether it is between the patients, doctors, their staff, or even between medical devices and diagnostic tools, there needs to be a robust, efficient line of communication in order to ensure the patients best interests. The internet and web communication is quickly becoming a cornerstone of that. The last few years only exacerbated this need and revealed how important remote communication can be in healthcare. I would be greatly interested in working within the healthcare industry in order to develop solutions that streamline these lines of communications. I believe that technology not only has the potential to make things more convenient for medical professionals, beyond that I am truly convinced that it can have a positive impact on morbidity and mortality, and improve clinical outcomes in general.
